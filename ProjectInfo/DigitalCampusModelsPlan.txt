Digital Campus Platform - Models Planning Document
=================================================

This document outlines the core data models needed for the IUB Digital Campus Project.

1. BASE USER MODEL
=================
Fields:
- id (string, unique identifier)
- username (string, unique)
- email (string, unique)
- firstName (string)
- lastName (string)
- role (string: student, faculty, admin, staff)
- status (string: active, inactive, suspended)
- createdAt (date)
- lastLogin (date)
- profile (object: avatar, bio, contact info)
- preferences (array: dashboard settings, notification preferences)
- phoneNumber (string)
- address (object: street, city, state, zip)
- dateOfBirth (date)
- emergencyContact (object: name, relationship, phone)
- ssoId (string: for single sign-on integration)
- mfaEnabled (boolean: for multi-factor authentication)
- lastPasswordChange (date)
- failedLoginAttempts (number)
- lockedUntil (date)

Methods:
- getFullName()
- updateLastLogin()
- updatePreferences(preferences)
- hasRole(role)
- isAdmin()
- isActive()
- deactivate()
- suspend()
- getDashboardWidgets()
- getPermissions()
- toJSON()
- fromJSON()

- advisorId (string: reference to faculty User)

- cumulativeGPA (number)
- libraryBooks (array: borrowed library books)
- libraryFines (number)
- libraryCardNumber (string)

Methods:
- addEnrollment(enrollment)
- addCourse(course)
- calculateGPA()
- toJSON()
- fromJSON()

Relationships:
- Base User model is extended by Student, Faculty, Admin, and Staff models
- Students enroll in courses
- Courses have instructors (faculty)
- Courses contain assignments
- Students submit assignments
- Assignments receive grades
- Grades contribute to overall course grades
- Users receive notifications
- Events can be associated with courses or users
- Enrollments track student progress in courses
- Students apply for financial aid
- Students borrow library books
- Users have emergency contacts
- Students participate in campus activities
- Faculty manage research projects

This model structure supports all the core features of the digital campus platform including:
- User authentication and role management
- Course registration and management
- Assignment distribution and submission
- Grade tracking and GPA calculation
- Communication through notifications
- Calendar and scheduling
- Dashboard personalization
- Financial aid tracking
- Library management
- Emergency contact management
- Campus activity participation
- Research project management
