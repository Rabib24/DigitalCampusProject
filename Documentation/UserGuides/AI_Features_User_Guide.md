## Overview

The Digital Campus system includes several AI-powered features designed to enhance your educational experience:

- **Intelligent Course Recommendations**: Personalized course suggestions based on your interests and academic history
- **Academic Performance Prediction**: Early insights into your potential performance in courses
- **Intelligent Search**: Advanced search capabilities with filtering options

These features use machine learning algorithms to provide personalized and relevant information to help you succeed in your academic journey.

## Intelligent Course Recommendations

### What it does
The recommendation system analyzes your academic history, interests, and goals to suggest courses that align with your educational path.

### How to access
1. Navigate to the "Recommendations" section in your dashboard
2. The system will automatically generate personalized recommendations
3. View details about each recommended course including why it was suggested

### Features
- **Personalized Suggestions**: Courses tailored to your academic interests
- **Match Scores**: Each recommendation includes a relevance score
- **Detailed Reasoning**: Explanation of why each course was recommended
- **Regular Updates**: Recommendations refresh based on your latest activities

### Interpreting Results
- **Match Score**: Higher scores (closer to 1.0) indicate stronger alignment with your interests
- **Reason**: Explanation of the factors that influenced the recommendation
- **Course Details**: Title, description, credits, and department information

## Academic Performance Prediction

### What it does
The performance prediction feature estimates your likely performance in courses before you enroll, helping you make informed decisions.

### How to access
1. Go to the course details page for any course you're considering
2. Look for the "Performance Prediction" section
3. View your predicted grade and confidence level

### Features
- **Grade Prediction**: Estimated letter grade for the course
- **Confidence Level**: How certain the system is in its prediction
- **Risk Assessment**: Identification of potential challenges
- **Success Factors**: Key elements that influence your predicted performance

### Interpreting Results
- **Predicted Grade**: Your estimated performance (e.g., A, B+, etc.)
- **Confidence Score**: Between 0.0 and 1.0, with higher values indicating more reliable predictions
- **Risk Level**: Low, Medium, or High risk of performance challenges
- **Factors**: Elements that contribute to the prediction (attendance, previous performance, etc.)

## Intelligent Search

### What it does
The intelligent search feature helps you find courses, documents, and discussions using natural language queries with advanced filtering.

### How to access
1. Use the main search bar at the top of any page
2. Enter your search query (e.g., "machine learning courses")
3. Apply filters to narrow down results

### Features
- **Natural Language Processing**: Understands context and intent in your queries
- **Advanced Filtering**: Filter by course type, department, credits, and relevance
- **Relevance Ranking**: Results ordered by how well they match your query
- **Quick Access**: Direct links to search results

### Search Filters
- **Type**: Filter by course, document, or forum
- **Department**: Narrow results to specific academic departments
- **Credits**: Show only courses with a minimum number of credits
- **Relevance**: Set a minimum relevance threshold for results

### Tips for Better Search Results
1. Use specific terms related to your interests
2. Try different combinations of keywords
3. Apply filters to narrow down large result sets
4. Check the relevance scores to focus on the most pertinent results

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Valid Digital Campus account
- Active enrollment in at least one course
- Up-to-date profile information

### Initial Setup
1. Complete your user profile with accurate information
2. Ensure your academic history is current
3. Review and update your interests and goals in the profile settings

### First Use
1. Visit the Recommendations dashboard to see your initial course suggestions
2. Check any course pages for performance predictions
3. Try searching for topics of interest to familiarize yourself with the search feature

### Best Practices
- Regularly update your profile as your interests evolve
- Provide feedback on recommendations to improve future suggestions
- Use performance predictions to identify areas for improvement
- Take advantage of search filters to find exactly what you need

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### No Recommendations Showing
- **Cause**: Insufficient academic history or profile information
- **Solution**: Complete your profile and ensure your academic records are up to date

#### Performance Predictions Seem Inaccurate
- **Cause**: Limited historical data or recent changes in circumstances
- **Solution**: Predictions improve over time as more data becomes available

#### Search Results Not Relevant
- **Cause**: Vague search terms or incorrect filters
- **Solution**: Use more specific keywords and adjust filters appropriately

### Contact Support
If you continue to experience issues with AI-powered features:
1. Document the specific problem you're encountering
2. Note any error messages displayed
3. Contact the Digital Campus support team through the Help Desk
4. Provide your user ID and a detailed description of the issue

### Providing Feedback
Your feedback helps improve these AI features:
1. Use the feedback buttons on recommendation and search result pages
2. Rate the usefulness of predictions and recommendations
3. Suggest improvements through the feedback portal

## Privacy and Data Usage

### Data Collection
The AI system uses the following information to provide personalized features:
- Academic history and performance
- Course enrollments and completions
- Profile information and stated interests
- Search queries and interactions

### Privacy Protections
- All data is anonymized before processing
- Personally identifiable information is protected
- Data is retained only as long as necessary for service provision
- You can request data deletion through the privacy portal

### Compliance
The system complies with:
- Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA)
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- Institutional data governance policies

For more information about privacy and data usage, please review the Digital Campus Privacy Policy.
